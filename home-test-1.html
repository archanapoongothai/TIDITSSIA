Welcome to TIDITSSIA

The Association was conceived by a group of Small Scale Industrialists and it was inaugurated 1st May 1967 at the historic city of Tiruchirapalli which is situated in the Central part of Tamil Nadu State, From day one it is serving the cause of Small Scale Industries of Tiruchirapalli District.

In India there are about 3.5 million small industries registered with government. They provide large employment to unskilled ,semiskilled and skilled manpower with a provision to constantly upgrade their skills. Mostly a single entrepreneur who arranges finance and will be in charge of production, marketing and manages such small industries.

There are few small industries depending upon the nature product, employ professional managers to take care of the activities. Hence all these industries are treated as a special category with several schemes of assistance from Government.
